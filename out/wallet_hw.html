Product description

The Archethic Cold Wallet is a revolutionary device designed to keep your digital assets safe. Using advanced biometric technology, it creates private keys from your fingerprint, ensuring that your biometric information is never stored, even on the device itself. This provides top-notch security and privacy, protecting you from the risks of traditional passwords.

If you lose the device, don't worry. You can easily recover your assets on a new Archethic device using your fingerprint. This means you are never dependent on a single device.

The Archethic Cold Wallet seamlessly connects with the Archethic blockchain. You can use it to process and secure transactions, making it an integral part of the Archethic ecosystem. It also supports multiple blockchains, allowing you to manage various cryptocurrencies from one place. This makes it perfect for anyone looking for a secure and user-friendly way to handle their digital assets.

Advantages of the Archethic Cold Wallet

Biometric Security and Privacy

Generates private keys directly from your fingerprint, ensuring top-notch security. Your biometric information is never stored, reducing the risk of breaches and misuse.

Robust and Recoverable

Easily recover assets on a new device using your fingerprint, minimizing dependency on a single device. If lost or stolen, use another Archethic device to access your finances.

Mass Adoption of Archethic

Integrates seamlessly with the Archethic blockchain, making each wallet user an Archethic blockchain user. Storing private keys as a keychain on the Archethic blockchain encourages adoption of the ecosystem and its dApps.

Blockchain Interoperability

Supports multiple blockchains, allowing unified management of various cryptocurrencies. Users can manage assets across different blockchains, including Archethic, providing the same interoperability as leading wallets like Ledger.

Access your private keys, your digital identity and your finances with the tip of your fingers.
